About Us

Description

The Young People is a ministry group of Mandurriao Fundamental Baptist Church with Ptr. Aaron Lev Java as the Youth Pastor.
We are one of the many Young People of different Churches around the globe.

Goals

Our goal is to spread to Gospel and make people learn more about our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ. We also have events such as ICF's, outing and Youth Hour to not only spend time with one another, but we would also remember that God is with us by giving some of our time for fellowship to Him.
